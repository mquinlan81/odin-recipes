A Taste of the Coast: Simple Shrimp Scampi

This classic Shrimp Scampi is a quick and easy dish that's perfect for a weeknight meal. Juicy shrimp are saut√©ed with garlic, butter, white wine, and lemon for a burst of Mediterranean flavor. Serve it over pasta, with crusty bread for dipping, or as an appetizer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season shrimp with salt and pepper.
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until pink and cooked through.
  5. Add white wine to the skillet and cook for 1 minute, or until the wine has reduced slightly.
  6. Stir in parsley and lemon juice.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Enjoy!
