Lasagna

A Layered Delight:

A Love Letter to Lasagna Lasagna. The very word conjures up images of warm, cheesy goodness, a symphony of flavors and textures that can feed a crowd or comfort a soul. This classic Italian dish is a true testament to the magic of layering tender pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and a blanket of melted mozzarella. It's a dish that requires a bit of time and effort, but the reward is immeasurable. This recipe focuses on a traditional meat-based lasagna, but feel free to experiment with variations. Add spinach or mushrooms to the meat sauce, use ground turkey or vegetables instead of beef, or try a lighter bechamel sauce. The possibilities are endless!

Ingredients:

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Bechamel Sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Nutmeg (optional)

For the Lasagna:

  • 1 pound dried lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Equipment:

  • Large pot
  • Skillet
  • 9x13 inch baking dish

Instructions:

Make the Meat Sauce:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  3. Drain off excess grease
  4. Add onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, oregano, basil, and red pepper flakes (if using)
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and cook for at least 30 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened

Make the Bechamel Sauce:

  1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat
  2. Whisk in flour and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly
  3. Gradually whisk in milk, stirring constantly to prevent lumps
  4. Bring to a simmer, stirring constantly, until the sauce has thickened.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg (if using)
  6. Remove from heat and set aside.

Assemble the Lasagna:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a large bowl, combine ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Layer 1/3 of the noodles over the sauce.
  7. Spread with 1/3 of the ricotta mixture.
  8. Top with 1/3 of the meat sauce and 1/3 of the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat layers two more times, ending with a layer of noodles and a final layer of mozzarella cheese.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  11. Uncover and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown.
  12. Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
  13. Enjoy!
