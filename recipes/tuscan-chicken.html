Creamy Tuscan Chicken

A Flavorful Journey: Creamy Tuscan Chicken

This Creamy Tuscan Chicken is a quick and easy weeknight meal that's bursting with flavor. Tender chicken breasts are simmered in a rich and creamy sauce made with sun-dried tomatoes, spinach, and heavy cream. Serve it over pasta, rice, or mashed potatoes for a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, packed in oil, drained and chopped
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 5-7 minutes per side, or until golden brown.
  4. Remove chicken breasts from the skillet and set aside.
  5. Add onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in diced tomatoes, sun-dried tomatoes, chicken broth, heavy cream, oregano, and basil.
  7. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  8. Return chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until heated through.
  9. Stir in spinach and cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Enjoy!
